# Community Guidelines

Welcome to TransHarbor! These guidelines help ensure everyone feels safe, respected, and welcome. Please read them carefully before participating.

**By using TransHarbor, you agree to follow these guidelines and help create a safe, supportive space for trans and nonbinary people.**

---

## Core Principles

### Trans and Nonbinary Centered
- This platform exists **for and by** trans and nonbinary people
- Allies and supporters are welcome when respectful and supportive
- Trans and nonbinary experiences are centered in all discussions

### Respect and Inclusivity
- **Treat everyone with dignity** regardless of their identity, journey, or background
- **Use correct names and pronouns** — if you're unsure, ask politely
- **Respect all trans identities** including binary, nonbinary, genderfluid, agender, and questioning individuals
- **Welcome newcomers** and help them feel at home

### Safety First
- **Zero tolerance** for harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- **No doxxing** or sharing personal information about others
- **Respect privacy** — don't share private conversations elsewhere
- **Report concerns** to moderators immediately via the report system

### Constructive Communication
- **Be kind and supportive** in your interactions
- **Disagree respectfully** when opinions differ
- **Avoid inflammatory topics** that could harm community wellbeing
- **Listen actively** and practice empathy
