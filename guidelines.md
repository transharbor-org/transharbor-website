# Community Guidelines

By using TransHarbor, you agree to follow these guidelines and help create a safe, supportive space for trans and nonbinary people.

## Core Principles

**Trans and Nonbinary Centered**
- Platform for and by trans and nonbinary people
- Allies welcome when respectful
- Trans and nonbinary experiences centered

**Respect and Inclusivity**
- Treat everyone with dignity
- Use correct names and pronouns
- Respect all trans identities
- Welcome newcomers

**Safety First**
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- No doxxing or sharing personal information
- Respect privacy
- Report concerns immediately

**Constructive Communication**
- Be kind and supportive
- Disagree respectfully
- Listen actively and practice empathy

## Prohibited

- Harassment, bullying, or threats
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Doxxing (sharing personal info without consent)
- Explicit sexual content
- Spam or scams
- Impersonation
- Platform abuse

## Reporting

Use the report button for violations. All reports reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, content removal, moderation strikes, suspension, or permanent ban.

**Contact:** support@transharbor.org
