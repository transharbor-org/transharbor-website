## Our Growing Community

TransHarbor started as a small Discord server and has grown into a thriving community of transgender individuals and allies from around the world. We're proud to provide:

- **Safe chat spaces** with active moderation
- **Peer support networks** for various life stages and experiences
- **Resource sharing** to help navigate transition and daily life
- **Community events** to bring us together
- **Crisis support** connections when needed

## What's Next

We're continuously working to improve and expand our community offerings:

- More specialized chat channels based on community feedback
- Regular virtual events and meetups
- Expanded resource sections with local information
- Better mobile experience for our website
- Community-driven content and guides
