Welcome to the TransHarbor website. We're a community chat platform for transgender individuals and allies.


## Getting Started

If you're new to TransHarbor:

1. **Read our [About page](/about)** to learn about our community
2. **Review our [Community Guidelines](/guidelines)** 
3. **Join our [Discord server](/chat)** - it's free and welcoming!
4. **Explore our [Resources](/resources)** for helpful information
5. **Introduce yourself** and become part of our supportive community

## Our Growing Community

- **Safe chat spaces** with active moderation
- **Peer support networks** for various life stages and experiences
- **Resource sharing** to help navigate transition and daily life
- **Community events** to bring us together
- **Crisis support** connections when needed

## Get Involved

TransHarbor is a community-driven initiative. Here's how you can help:

- **Join our Discord** and participate in conversations
- **Share resources** that have helped you
- **Welcome new members** and help them feel at home  
- **Provide feedback** on how we can improve
- **Consider volunteering** as we grow
